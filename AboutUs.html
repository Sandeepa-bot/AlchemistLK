Welcome to Alchemist in Sri Lanka! Launched in 2024 by Charitha Dissanayake, our YouTube channel is your ultimate destination for exploring the rich tapestry of Sri Lankan history, mythology, cricket, celebrity culture, and film criticism. Whether you're a history buff, a cricket enthusiast, a fan of celebrity interviews, or a movie aficionado, we have something for everyone. Our mission is to delve into the fascinating stories that shape Sri Lanka, from ancient legends and historical events to the glory days of cricket and contemporary cinema. Join us as we uncover the magic and mysteries of Sri Lanka, one captivating story at a time.
